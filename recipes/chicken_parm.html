Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken parm is a dish that consists of breaded chicken breast covered in tomato sauce and mozzarella, parmesean, or provolone cheese.This dish originated from the 20th-century Italian diaspora.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 (16 oz) package skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray. Place chicken on the prepared pan.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, flipping chicken after 12 minutes. Remove from the oven, add desired amount of sauce, and top each breast with mozzarella cheese.
  3. Bake until cheese is melted and chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 12 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
